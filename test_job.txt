Senior Product Manager - Growth

About the Company:
We're a fast-growing B2B SaaS company helping teams collaborate better. Our mission is to empower knowledge workers to do their best work through intuitive, delightful software.

About the Role:
We're looking for a Senior Product Manager to own our Growth team's roadmap. You'll work closely with design, engineering, data, and marketing to drive user acquisition, activation, and retention.

Responsibilities:
• Develop and execute product strategy for user growth and engagement
• Lead cross-functional teams (design, engineering, data science) to ship features that drive key metrics
• Conduct user research and analyze data to identify opportunities and validate hypotheses
• Run A/B tests and experiments to optimize conversion funnels
• Partner with marketing and sales to ensure product-market fit
• Present insights and recommendations to executive leadership
• Build and maintain growth roadmap aligned with company OKRs

Required Skills & Experience:
• 5+ years of product management experience
• Proven track record of driving user engagement and growth metrics
• Strong data analysis skills (SQL, analytics tools like Looker, Mixpanel, or similar)
• Experience with A/B testing and experimentation frameworks
• Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills
• Experience working with less technical stakeholders
• Ability to balance qualitative user research with quantitative data

Preferred:
• Experience in B2B SaaS or education technology
• Background working with globally distributed teams
• Pragmatic Marketing certification
• Experience presenting to C-level executives

What We Offer:
• Competitive salary and equity
• Remote-first culture with optional office space
• Professional development budget
• Mission-driven team focused on meaningful work
